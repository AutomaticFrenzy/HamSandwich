To run the game, you must provide your own copy of the installer, either retail or downloaded from itch.io.

Randomizer Info

The difficulty can be changed in options and must be set before starting.

Go to the Randomizer menu item. Type in a seed or leave blank. Hit generate and wait for a number to show up. hit Play!

From now on, any time you hit play it will start this seed, and saving and loading works, and persists when reloading the webpage.

Hit Export save at the top to get a zip with a spoiler log if you are stuck.
